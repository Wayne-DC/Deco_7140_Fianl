Activity

People will learn how to begin riding a horse and how to take care for the horse during times of health and illness. In addition, horseback riding teaches patience, discipline, understanding, empathy, compassion, self-control, and dedication!

People will learn different knowledge of fishing and fish, how to begin fishing by the guidance of the coach. Besides, if you are not interested in fishing, we can provide a boat for you, people can drive the boat on the river to enjoy the quiet time!

This is a great event for the family trip; people can take a carriage with your family tour around the whole town. The professional drivers will take care of you and your family's safety. Besides, some traditional snacks and beers(you must be 21 years old or above!) offered in unlimited quantities on the trip.

If you are passionate about the old west culture, this is an excellent opportunity. people can dress like cowboys or cowgirls to feel living in the old west. Besides, if you like the style of old fashion, you are welcome to buy some traditional clothes or take some souvenirs for your family or friends.
